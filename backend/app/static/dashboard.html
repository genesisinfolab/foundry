Strategy Description
Newman Strategy · Rules-based trendline breakout system for small-cap sector rotation · Paper mode active
Overview
The Newman Strategy targets asymmetric situations in small-cap and micro-cap stocks: a stock with a clean share structure, a genuine sector catalyst, and a multi-year resistance trendline that is about to break. The expected win rate is below 50%. The edge comes entirely from the win/loss ratio — winners are expected to be 10× the size of losers. One 50% winner covers ten 5% stop-outs and still produces a profit.
All trading decisions — entry, sizing, pyramiding, and exit — are driven by deterministic rules. No discretionary overrides. No averaging down. The system is designed to be early into a theme, size up only after confirmation, and exit instantly when a position stops acting right.
The Four Corners — Entry Conviction Framework
Every entry requires at least 2 of 4 corners to be active. Sizing follows conviction level.
📈
Chart
Multi-year downtrend trendline resistance break. Close exceeds the declining line through historical peak highs by ≥ 1%. Not a 20-day breakout — a structural break.
🏗️
Structure
Volume surge ≥ 2.5× the 20-day average. Institutions and speculators are loading. Float < 200M — small float amplifies price movement on volume.
🌐
Sector
Stock is within 20% of its 52-week high. The sector is alive, ETF peers are moving, and this name is in breakout territory — not dead in the water.
⚡
Catalyst
A news or regulatory event tied to the theme — FDA approval, legislation, government contract, earnings surprise. The why behind the move.
7-Step Execution Pipeline
1
Theme Detection
Scan news (Finnhub, Perigon), social (Reddit, Twitter), and 30+ specialized ETFs for emerging sector signals. Score: news × 0.4 + social × 0.3 + ETF × 0.3. Identify the theme before mainstream media.
2
Watchlist Building
For each hot theme, find candidate stocks via ETF holdings, Finnhub industry peers, and keyword symbol search. Enrich with price, float, and volume from Alpaca and Alpha Vantage.
3
Share Structure Filter
Keep only stocks with float < 200M shares, price > $0.50, avg daily volume > 100k. Rank by float size (smaller = better) and volume. Eliminate diluted, illiquid, or sub-penny names.
4
Breakout Scanner
Detect the trendline resistance break on 252 bars of history. Score conviction across four corners. Gate on SPY bull regime (20-bar change > +2%). Signal only if conviction ≥ 2.
5
Shotgun Entry
Open a $2,500 starter position — always small regardless of conviction. Set ATR-based stop at entry − 1.5 × ATR (min 5% below entry). Launch immediately-wrong monitor for first 15 minutes.
6
Pyramiding
Scale in only after the stock proves itself. Four tiers: +3% → add 2% portfolio, +8% → add 5%, +15% → add 10%, +25% → add 10%. Max 4 levels. Max 35% single stock, 60% per theme.
7
Risk Management
ATR stop enforced continuously. Uptrend trendline exit as primary managed exit. Profit tiers at 15% / 30% / 45% as fallback. Kill switch blocks all new entries on pause. 24-hour cooldown after any stop-out.
Exit Priority Hierarchy
#1 Immediately Wrong Within first 15 min: price drops > 1×ATR below entry → full exit. No waiting, no averaging.
#2 Hard ATR Stop Close ≤ stop price (entry − 1.5×ATR) → exit at next open. Position enters 24h cooldown.
#3 Uptrend Trendline Break Close breaks below the rising support line (mirror of entry signal). Must be in profit and ≥ 6 bars old. Signals the trend is structurally over.
#4 Profit Tier Scale-Out +15% → sell 33%. +30% → sell 33% more. +45% → sell remaining. Fallback when trendline hasn't formed yet.
#5 Media Saturation When mainstream media broadly covers the story, the edge is gone. Exit before the crowd exits.
Position Sizing Rules
  • Starter$2,500 flat — always the same size regardless of conviction. Size is earned, not assumed. The starter is the permission slip to add more.
  • Max Stock35% of portfolio maximum in any single name at any pyramid level. Prevents ruin from a single position.
  • Max Theme60% of portfolio maximum in any one sector theme. Avoids sector-correlated blow-ups.
  • Risk / Trade5% of current equity risked per trade in the sequential equity model. Compound sizing grows with the account, shrinks after drawdowns.
  • Cooldown24-hour lock on any symbol after a stop-out. Prevents re-entering a broken trade on emotion.
Core Philosophy
Win rate is irrelevant
The system is designed for <50% win rate with a 10:1 W/L ratio. One 50% winner covers ten 5% losses with profit left over.
Be early, not right
Enter before mainstream coverage. Exit when mainstream coverage arrives. The crowd's arrival signals the end of the edge.
Four corners before size
The $2,500 starter is a hypothesis. Pyramids are reserved for confirmed winners. Never add to a position that isn't working.
Instant exit, zero grief
If a stock doesn't act right in the first 15 minutes or breaks the stop, exit immediately. There is no averaging down. There is no hope trade.
Preserve the core
The account is the ammunition. Never risk it on a single theme or name. Diversify across sectors. Follow all position limits without exception.
Process over outcome
A losing trade that followed every rule is a success. A winning trade that broke a rule is a failure. Judge process, not individual results.
SPY Regime Gate
All new entries are blocked unless SPY's 20-bar (20-day) price change exceeds +2%. This prevents entering breakouts into a deteriorating broad market where sector rotation is reversing. No breakouts in bear tape.
